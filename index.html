Miami Royal Ballet School

At our ballet studio in Miami, the school creates a solid foundation and prepares students to dance all types of classical ballet and modern dances. Our ballet class program is designed to take students from their very first step through a comprehensive curriculum of classical ballet studies. It also encourages physical discipline and general body awareness and control. It helps understand the relationship between music, rhythm and controlled movement.

Ballet Classes - Adults and Children

  • Creative Movement 1 and 2
  • Pre-ballet 1 and 2
  • Ballet 1, 2 and 3
  • Pointe
  • Broadway Jazz
  • Flamenco
  • Private classes
  • Ballet workout
