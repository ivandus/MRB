Ballet School Rules and Regulations

Rules and Regulations

The school year begins August 29th 2011 and ends June 2012

Registration

Registration form must be signed in advanced in order to take a class. There is an annual registration fee of $50.00

Tuition 

Children Beginner Ballet Class: 1 hour per week $90.00 per month.
Junior and Intermediate Ballet Classes: 1 hour twice a week $125.00 per month.
Advance Ballet Class: 1 1/2 hour twice a week (includes pointe) $190.00 per month.
Adult Ballet Class: 1 hour twice a week $120.00 per month.
Broadway Jazz Class: Children and Adults 1 1/2 hour per week $100.00.
Lyrics Class: 1 hour per week. Children $90.00
Ballet Workout: 1 hour twice a week $120.00 per month.
Salsa Packages: 4 Classes: $60.00      8 Classes: $96.00
Salsa Couples:   4 Classes: $48.00      8 Classes: $80.00
Zumba: Walk in: $12.00      8 Classes: $80.00
Private Classes: 1 hour twice a week $60.00 per class. 1 hour more than two classes per week $45.00 per class.
Ballet try out or Walk in Classes: $25.00 per class.
Multi-class Discount: Price discount applys for Flamenco and Jazz classes for students taking Ballet classes as well.


Absences or holidays do not excuse payments for monthly tuition. No credits are given for absences. If your child misses a class, he/she can make up the class within the same week. Tuition payment must be received by the 10th of every month. There will be a $15.00 late fee for payments received after the 15th of the month and a returned check fee of $25.00.
Make all tuition payments payable to:
Miami Royal Ballet, Inc.

Punctuality

Classes will begin promptly at their designated times. For the purpose of maintaining discipline, students must arrive five minutes earlier before class and must be picked up promptly after class has ended. We are not responsible for any student leaving the premises with out permission.

Dance Attire

The students must wear the complete designated ballet attire in every class: Leotard, pink tights, pink or black ballet sweaters (during winter months) and ballet shoes. Important: Hair must be neatly secured at the back of the face and up in the neck.

Photo / Media Release

Occasionally, throughout the classes or school events, participants of all ages might be photographed or videotaped. These images may be used in promotions and posting of pictures on the school website or in publications that are distributed to the public for promotional and informational purposes.
